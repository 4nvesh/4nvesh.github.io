Featured Project
Tars - Smart Planter
Did you know that there are certain plants that need special care to survive and grow?
Tars is a product of the Internet of Things. With the help of some sensors like soil moisture, temperature and sunlight detection sensors, it keeps you updated about the current conditions of your beloved plant.The web application helps you monitor the soil moisture, temperature & humidity of the surroundings, sunlight and weather conditions to help you take better care of your plant. When there is something wrong with the plant or its environment, it alerts you to the issue.
